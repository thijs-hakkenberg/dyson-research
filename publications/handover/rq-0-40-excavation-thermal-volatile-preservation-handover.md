## Key Conclusions from External Research

**The volatile preservation picture is now well-characterized:**
1. **Bennu is volatile-rich** — more so than Ryugu, with CI chondrite composition
2. **Optical mining** is a viable alternative to mechanical excavation that inherently manages thermal extraction
3. **Mechanical excavation** requires thermal management (shading, rapid processing) to preserve volatiles
4. **The physics is well-understood** from Metzger's model + Bennu ground-truth data
